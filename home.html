About Us

On Complementor, meet with people who inspire and challenge you to think differently.

Our solution is a complete mentoring service, matching and facilitating the mentoring relationship. Complementor uses an algorithm to match complementary pairs, using personality types, skills and goals.

We want to help empower people to achieve their personal and career goals within industry, whilst aiding companies to solve their diverse recruitment issues.

  • Mentoring is the best way to encourage diversity.
  • Our project benefits mentors, mentees and companies. By hosting mentoring online we make it accessible to a wider range of participants. We plan to partner with companies, universities and outreach programs to build our network.
  • Many mentoring schemes match based on shared characteristics. By focusing on matching different people we offer a unique experience. Combining personality testing, skills, goals and industry experience we are able to match mentors and mentees.

Frequently Asked Questions

If you have any further questions, please don't hesitate to contact us.

  • How innovative is this solution or its execution?

    Many mentoring schemes match based on shared characteristics. By focusing on matching different people we offer a unique experience. Combining personality testing, skills, goals and industry experience we are able to match mentors and mentees.

  • How inclusive is this project? Who can it help or benefit?

    Our project benefits mentors, mentees and companies. By hosting mentoring online we make it accessible to a wider range of participants. We plan to partner with companies, universities and outreach programs to build our network.

  • How do I sign up?

    The process is very simple - click on any of the "sign up" links on the homepage, register using your email address, then proceed to the questionaires. Simple!

  • How does the process work?

    For mentees, simply sign up, complete the personality test, tell us about your interests, goals and location and we will pair you with 3 potential mentors. You may then choose your preference, and our integrated chat service will allow you to reach out.

    For mentors, simply sign up, complete the personality test, tell us about your interests, goals and location and potential mentees will reach out to you. We will also offer guidance on mentoring within the profile area.

  • Why should my company sponsor you?

    Great question! We rely on company sponsorship, so supporting our cause is a great way to support a good cause. If you sign up as a corporate sponsor, you can receive access to young, diverse talent through the mentorship scheme, and use our tool for recruitment purposes.
